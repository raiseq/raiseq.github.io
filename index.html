  • Frameworks/Libaries: Flask, Jinja, jQuery, Bootstrap, Sass, ReactJS, MeteorJS, Laravel, Blade, VueJS
  • Database/Industry Tools: PostgreSQL, Mongo, SQLAlchemy (ORM), Git, Command Line

With a background in marketing, business management, and web development, I am a marketer turned software engineer who designs and develops web applications from start to finish.

Programming has always been on and off in my life. As a kid, I built websites for fun as a hobby with just HTML, CSS, and Notepad. In university, I took a few introductory computer science courses and excelled at them. Revisiting this interest in design and programming, I was self-taught before attending and completing an accelerated software engineering program at Hackbright Academy.

Now I am excited to leverage my business background and passion for technology into a full-stack software engineering role.

When I'm not busy coding, I'm eating and sleeping. Food makes me happy ♥
